# Module 2: Identity & Access Management (IAM)

## Learning Objectives

By the end of this module, you will be able to:

- Understand IAM fundamentals (users, groups, roles, policies)
- Create and manage service accounts
- Assign IAM roles to users, groups, and service accounts
- Understand IAM policy hierarchy
- Configure organization policies
- Troubleshoot IAM permission issues

## Module Overview

This module covers **Domain 5** of the ACE exam (15% of the exam), focusing on configuring access and security through IAM.

## Topics Covered

1. **IAM Fundamentals**
   - Users, groups, and service accounts
   - Roles (primitive, predefined, custom)
   - IAM policies and bindings
   - Policy hierarchy

2. **Service Accounts**
   - Creating and managing service accounts
   - Service account keys
   - Impersonation
   - Best practices

3. **IAM Roles and Policies**
   - Understanding role types
   - Assigning roles
   - Custom roles
   - Policy evaluation

4. **Organization Policies**
   - What are organization policies
   - Common policy constraints
   - Policy inheritance
